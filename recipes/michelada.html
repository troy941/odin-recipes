Michelada

A michelada is a Mexican drink made with beer, lime juice, assorted sauces (often chili-based), spices, and chili peppers. It is served in a chilled, salt-rimmed, glass.

This drink is perfect for day-drinking in the yard or by the pool. To make it extra yummy, rim the glass with tajin instead of salt.

Ingredients

  • One or two lime wedges
  • Salt
  • Tajin
  • Ice cubes
  • Lime juice
  • Whatever hot sauce you think is best
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Maggi sauce seasoning
  • Some ice cold Mexican lager

Instructions

  1. Rim the edges of your glass with the lime wedges.
  2. Dip the limed rim of the glass first into the salt and then into the tajin.
  3. Add half a cup of ice to the glass.
  4. Add the lime juice, Maggi Sauce, Tabasco Sauce, and Worcestershire sauce and mix with a spoon
  5. Crack open your lager and pour it slowly into your glass.
  6. Take as needed for pain.
