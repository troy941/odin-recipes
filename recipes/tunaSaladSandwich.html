Tuna Salad Sandwich

This is a tuna salad sandwich! It's a all-American diner classic. Cool, creamy, and refreshing tuna salad sandwiched in between two slices of toast to create a... a sandwich.

Coffee, candy, ketchup, mustard, napkins, sugar packets, and checkered backdrop not included. Chicken salad is not the opposite of tuna salad!

Ingredients

Part I: tuna salad

  • Canned tuna
  • One small onion
  • One small bell pepper
  • 80ish grams of unflavored, unsweetened Greek yogurt
  • Paprika
  • Lemon juice (the secred ingredient!)
  • Your favorite hot sauce

Part II: sandwich

  • Two slices of bread
