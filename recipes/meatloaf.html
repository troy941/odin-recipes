Meatloaf

We all love meatloaf. It is delicious and yummy! It is a meaty meal to share with friends, family, and maybe even your...dog?

Note: this dish contains meat and gluten.

Ingredients

The LOAF

  • Meat (we recommend using beef)
  • Bread crumbs (stale is best)
  • diced onion
  • eggs (remove the shell first)
  • dried parsley
  • garlic power
  • salt n peppa

The SAUCE

  • ketchup
  • worcestershire sauce
  • hot sauce of your choice (secret ingredient)

Instructions
